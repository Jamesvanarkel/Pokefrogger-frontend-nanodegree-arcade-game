A simple frogger Game build in canvas and JS. The game works by pressing the arrow buttons to move the character.

How to play: 1. Use your arrow keys to move around the character. 2. Don't get hit by the Pokémon shooting from left to right. 3. Grab the Pokéballs before you get hit. 4. Level up!
